About Me

Hi, my name's Lexi Guches! I'm a double major at Eastern Washington University in Computer Science and 3D Animation/Game Design. I love the problem solving and logic puzzles involved in programming, just as I love designing and being able to express myself and ideas in image form.

Ideally, I want to work in the gaming industry, preferably as a character designer. The challenge of creating something interesting, while having the play to the strengths and systems of the physics engine and programming code is its biggest appeal.

In my spare time, I love excercising and food! Nutrition and health have always been an interest of mine, and I try my best to eat clean, excercise regularly, and get lots of sleep (which can be hard given my schedule!) I'm always out to gain new knowledge and try to learn at least one new fact each day: Did you know that the reason purple was considered a royal color was that it took thousands of deep-sea snails to dye one garment?
